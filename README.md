# Solar Earth

A Pebble watchface showing a day/night globe behind the time, with the terminator tracking the real sun and city lights glowing after dark.
Pick your region, choose the info lines (date, weather, sunrise/sunset, steps…), and toggle the solar ring around the edge.
Globe day/night shading is inspired by Joshua Simmons' **Blue Pebble**; the clock layout, solar ring and widgets are inspired by Freakified's **Halcyon**.
