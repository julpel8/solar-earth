# Solar Earth

A Pebble watchface showing a day/night globe behind the time. The terminator follows the real position of the sun, and the night side shows city lights after dark.

**Features**

- **6 regions** — center the globe on Europe, Africa, North or South America, Asia, or Oceania.
- **Configurable info lines** — set what sits above and below the time: date, weather, sunrise/sunset, steps, distance, heart rate, battery, a second & third time zone, and more. Order, grouping and per-line text size are adjustable.

Globe day/night shading is inspired by Joshua Simmons' **Blue Pebble**; the clock layout and widgets are inspired by Freakified's **Halcyon**.
